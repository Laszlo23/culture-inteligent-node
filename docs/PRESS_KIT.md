## One-sentence pitch

Culture Node is a Proof of Attention OS that turns real learning into knowledge fuel for a Solana Devnet node — now with Hearing Mode so you can mine culture without looking at a screen.

## 50-word blurb

Collectibles already won attention. Culture Node mines what sticks: Attention Academy sessions become Proof of Attention, which fuels your node. Culture Club is the membership ritual — sign, learn, spread love and knowledge. Hearing Mode makes the whole loop ears-first. Built by Building Culture. Live on Devnet.

## Duality + Zen (product spine)

Culture Node keeps **Mind ↔ Machine**. Zen Mode is knowledge-first: when an Academy exercise is ready, the human learning loop **stops** and becomes a decision — **Mind** (hold the knowledge) or **Machine** (convert attention → fuel via Neural Snap). Hearing Mode voice: say *Zen*, then *Mind* or *Machine*.

**Hook Mirror (Proof of Hook Awareness):** after First Spark, members name (1) what hooks them, (2) what they notice when doomscrolling again, (3) why they keep going — then Zen-decide. That is the doomscroll moment made provable.

## Hearing Mode lead story

**Headline angle:** Culture Node ships a screenless Proof of Attention OS — mine culture with your ears.

Culture Node just went ears-first. Hearing Mode uses the browser’s speech engine so members can hear facility status, run Attention Academy quizzes by voice, and navigate with commands like *Help*, *Academy*, *Status*, and *Spread* — without staring at a dashboard. The product claim stays the same: attention becomes fuel. The interface is optional.
