## Rules that keep the community human

1. **Free core stays free** — First Spark + Hearing never behind a paywall.
2. **No extractive “join for airdrop”** — invite to prove attention, not farm points.
3. **Founder answers for 7 days** — every newcast / new joiner gets a human reply.
4. **One ritual > ten features** — weekly Hearing demo beats another room in the facility.
5. **Measure Spreads → return** — vanity MAU without return is noise.
